Heinz × Deadpool & Wolverine
"Can't Unsee It"

Heinz leveraged the global release of Deadpool & Wolverine by pointing out the obvious: Deadpool's red suit = Ketchup. Wolverine's yellow = Mustard. Launched under #NowYouCantUnseeit.

My Role: Media Planner at dentsu — contributed to channel strategy, audience targeting, and media buys during the film's peak theatrical window.

OREO × Marvel
"Stuf of Legends"

Launched February 2, 2026, the largest special-edition drop in OREO history and the first-ever partnership uniting Avengers, Spider-Man, X-Men, and Fantastic Four. 32 unique character embossments, first-ever color-changing crème, and pack art by Marvel artist Todd Nauck.

A three-act story: presale, Feb 2 retail launch, and a March 2 villain-themed "Stuf of Doom" Doctor Doom drop.

Sources: PR Newswire · Dentsu Creative

Canada Soccer · Experiential · Toronto · April 2026

Canada Soccer
"Our Game Now" Jersey Swap

Ahead of the 2026 FIFA World Cup, Canada Soccer hosted a jersey swap outside Café Diplomatico in Toronto's Little Italy. Italian-Canadian fans lined up to trade Italy jerseys for Canada's new "Our Game Now" kits.

Coverage from CP24, TSN, Toronto Star, and 6ixBuzzTV, trending across social with over 10K engagements.
